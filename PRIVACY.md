# Privacy Policy
Effective date: 2026-01-26

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how EasyCRM (“the App”) is provided by Joseph Du (“we,” “us,” or “our”). It applies to the use of the App and its features within the App.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect the following categories of information:

### Account Information
- Email address (used for login)
- Authentication identifiers (Firebase Authentication)

### User Content
- Tasks
- Clients
- Files
- Email templates
- Profile avatar (if uploaded)

### Device / Technical Data
We do not collect device or technical analytics data (e.g., no Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics).

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
- Authenticate and manage your account
- Provide App functionality (including managing tasks, clients, files, and templates)
- Store your profile avatar in Firebase Storage
- Protect the App and diagnose issues when necessary, using limited technical information available during normal operation

## 4. Data Storage & Security
Your data is stored locally on your device and/or securely on Firebase (Google Cloud). Access is restricted by authentication. Avatars and user data are protected by access rules. We do not sell or share personal data.

## 5. Data Sharing
We do not share data with third parties except infrastructure providers necessary to operate the App, such as Firebase (Google Cloud).

## 6. Data Retention & Deletion
You can delete your account in the App. Upon deletion, associated data is removed within a reasonable period.

## 7. Your Rights
You may, subject to applicable laws:
- Access your information
- Update your information
- Request deletion of your information
- Contact us with questions or requests

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the effective date when changes are made.
